# How to Add Your Profile Image

## Quick Setup

1. **Save your image** as `profile.jpg` in the portfolio folder
   - Location: `C:\Users\boss\Desktop\portfolio\profile.jpg`
   - Recommended size: 400x400px (square image works best)
   - Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP

2. **That's it!** The portfolio will automatically display your image

---

## Supported Formats
- `.jpg` / `.jpeg`
- `.png` 
- `.webp`
- `.gif`

---

## Image Tips for Best Results
- Use a professional headshot (face centered)
- Good lighting is essential
- Solid background recommended
- Minimum 300x300px resolution
- Maximum file size: 500KB

Simply replace `profile.jpg` with your new image and refresh the browser.
